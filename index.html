STORY
WHO WE ARE

Our Story

SURVIVORS was founded on April 2, 2025 by Alexuu01 — built from scratch with one vision: unite Romania's best Free Fire talent under a single flag.

APR 2, 2025

The Beginning

Alexuu01 founded SURVIVORS alone — scouting players from the MEL ROMANIA league, created and sponsored by Infinite Gaming 🇷🇴, Romania's professional Counter-Strike organisation. He personally messaged each player he believed in, handpicking the squad one by one.

LATE 2025

The Pause

The project was paused after a difficult period. But the dream never disappeared. The fire kept burning — waiting for the right moment to strike back.

MAY 6, 2026

The Rebirth ⚡

ECEL FREE FIRE ESPORTS — an organisation representing 🇩🇪 Germany & 🇷🇴 Romania — chose this squad as their official roster. Financial stability. Professional coaches. A real structure.
The dream finally had a home.

Every member was handpicked by Alexuu01 — scouted from MEL ROMANIA, the league built and backed by Infinite Gaming.

After a period of uncertainty, ECEL FREE FIRE ESPORTS gave the squad something bigger: an official home, financial security, and access to professional coaching — all while representing both 🇩🇪 Germany and 🇷🇴 Romania on the European stage.

Every setback led here. Every sacrifice made sense.
Now we compete for real.

"

We are not just a team.

— SURVIVORS / ECEL ESPORTS

Our Mission

To represent Germany and Romania on the European stage — competing under the ECEL FREE FIRE ESPORTS banner with the structure, support and ambition to reach the very top.
